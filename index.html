WHAT IS BREAKDOWN SERVICE?

Breakdown Service is a type of insurance that ensures you receive help when your vehicle breaks down due to a flat battery, clutch issues, or engine problem. If you are unable to get your vehicle moving, a trained professional will be sent to assist you. If on-the-spot repairs are not possible, your car will be towed to the nearest garage or your chosen destination .
